AI Research Scientist

Elevate Labs operates at the intersection of deep research and deployable engineering, we develop proprietary ventures and partner with select enterprises to solve their most complex systemic challenges.

About the position

We are looking for a specialist to operationalize "Responsible AI" within our R&D process. You will ensure that our models comply with global regulations and align with our internal ethical standards. You will not just be a compliance checker, but a strategic advisor who ensures our technology remains viable in the regulated global market.

Job responsibilities

  • Audit algorithms and datasets for bias, fairness, and potential societal risks.
  • Monitor evolving global AI regulations (EU AI Act, GDPR, etc.) and ensure our ventures remain compliant.
  • Develop internal frameworks and "Red Teaming" protocols to stress-test models for safety and security.
  • Collaborate with engineering teams to implement privacy-preserving technologies (e.g., differential privacy).
  • Draft transparency reports and ethical usage guidelines for our partners and clients.

Requirements

  • Deep understanding of the EU AI Act, GDPR, CCPA, and emerging global AI governance frameworks.
  • Experience using tools and methodologies for detecting bias and fairness in datasets (e.g., AIF360).
  • Background in enterprise risk assessment, compliance, or legal engineering.
  • Ability to understand high-level model architectures and converse fluently with engineering teams.
  • Exceptional ability to translate complex technical risks into executive-level strategy.
