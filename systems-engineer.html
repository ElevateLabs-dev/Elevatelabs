Systems Engineer

Elevate Labs operates at the intersection of deep research and deployable engineering, we develop proprietary ventures and partner with select enterprises to solve their most complex systemic challenges.

About the position

We are looking for a Systems Engineer to architect the distributed infrastructure that powers our ventures. This is a role for an engineer who understands low-level system design, concurrency, and fault tolerance. You will ensure that solutions maintain our 99.99% reliability standard even under heavy computational load.

Job responsibilities

  • Architect and maintain high-performance distributed systems using languages such as Go, Rust, or C++.
  • Design infrastructure for fault tolerance, ensuring no single point of failure in our critical proprietary services.
  • Optimize database interactions and caching layers to minimize system latency.
  • Conduct rigorous load testing and "adversarial engineering" to identify system bottlenecks before they impact operations.
  • Manage container orchestration (Kubernetes) and cloud-native networking configurations.

Requirements

  • Expert-level proficiency in Rust, C++, or Go.
  • Deep understanding of concurrency, CAP theorem, consistency models, and consensus algorithms (Raft/Paxos).
  • Experience tuning high-performance databases (PostgreSQL, Redis, Cassandra) at the engine level.
  • Strong grasp of OS fundamentals, kernel tuning, and networking protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP/2, QUIC).
  • Proficiency with Terraform or Ansible for defining immutable infrastructure.
